Web Hosting Project

1. Bluehost

Bluehost is a great and affordable web hosting site. The first hosting service is the shared web hosting service that starts at a price of $3.95 a month.

Here is some of the services included in this plan:

  • 1 Website
  • 50 GB SSD Storage
  • Unmetered Bandwidth
  • Free SSL Certificate
  • Standard performance
  • 1 Included Domain
  • 5 Parked Domains
  • 25 Subdomains

The second service that they offer is a dedicated hosting service starting at $79.99 a month.

Here is a list of the services included in the dedicated hosting plan:

  • 4 Cores at 2.3 GHz
  • 500 GB Mirrored Storage
  • 4 GB Ram
  • 5 TB Bandwidth
  • 3 IP Adresses

The Third service that they offer is a VPS hosting service starting at $19.99 a month.

Here is a list of the services included in the VPS hosting service:

  • 2 Cores
  • 30 GB SSD Storage
  • 2 GB RAM
  • 1 TB Bandwidth
  • 1 IP Address
